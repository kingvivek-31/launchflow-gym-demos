Welcome to the
Living Experience

At Coreflex, we believe movement is for every body. Our studio is an inclusive, welcoming space where you come as you are and leave feeling more connected — to yourself, to others, and to the joy of moving.

Whether you're taking your very first class or deepening an existing practice, our expert instructors hold space for your unique journey with warmth, expertise, and heart.
